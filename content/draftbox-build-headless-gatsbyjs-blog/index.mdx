## How does it work?

To build your blog Draftbox gives you option to select your data source either form WordPress (both cloud and self-hosted supported ) or Ghost platform once you select it also offer you prebuilt Gatsbyjs theme for frontend user interface and build the blog for you and host it to your Netlify service account.

## A blazing fast Gatsbyjs theme

Draftbox offer you super fast rich Gatsbyjs theme to display your blog posts beautifully.
